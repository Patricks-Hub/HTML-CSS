Bright Futures

Many Iowa-educated engineers and scientists have contributed to NASA missions and private space companies.

A Rich History Of Stars

The University of Iowa has a rich history in space research, with renowned scientists like James Van Allen making significant contributions to our understanding of the Earth's radiation belts.

One Small Step

The Ames Research Center, a major NASA facility, is located in California's Silicon Valley but has strong ties to Iowa State University.

Launching The Future

The Iowa Space Grant Consortium, a NASA-funded program, supports space-related research, education, and outreach activities at various Iowa institutions, fostering the next generation of space scientists and engineers.
